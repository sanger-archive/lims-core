Overview

The Core of the LIM System. Includes the basic classes corresponding to the :

  1. domain(s)

  2. Persistence Layer

  3. Business Logic layer (extension ?)

The Core is split in the following submodule/namespace :

  1. Laboratory : Things used/found in the lab. Includes pure laboratory (inert things as plates, tubes) and chemical one (as aliquots, samples).

  2. LabProcess Related to the work people/robot do in the laboratories.

  3. Organization Related to users, data release (studies) and ordering (orders) and funding (projects) etc.

  4. Actions High level actions that can be performed on things (mostly laboratory).

  5. Persistence

This partition is more for clarity/documentation purposes and it's not meant to be really tight. However it's more likely than the submodules dependency will be a tree than a graph, (but it's not a necessity).
